Walrus

Walruses have long fusks and a prominent mustache.These large marine mammals are found near the Artic Cicle.They are extremely social and snort and bellow loudly at their companions. During the mating season they are quite aggresive. Walruses have an air sac under their throat,which helps them float in the water so they can sleep!
